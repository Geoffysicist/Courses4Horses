Change to Running Time Mode

    Press Setup
    Press Prev Choice until you see Exit Setup
    Press Eyes On
    Press Next Choice until you see Printer Type
    Press Enter
    Press Next Choice until you see ASCII Fixed Pt
    Press Enter
    Press Next Choice until you see 9600 Baud
    Press Enter

Change to Normal Mode

    Press Setup
    Press Prev Choice until you see Exit Setup
    Press Eyes On
    Press Next Choice until you see Printer Type
    Press Enter
    Press Next Choice until you see Standard Output
    Press Next Choice until you see 1200 Baud
    Press Enter

Make sure you choose ASCII Fixed Pt and not ASCII Float Pt
